Front End
---------

  [ Theme ]
    I used OCHIISHI Koichiro's "Hugo Zen" theme as a starting point and 
    modified it to my tastes. I added top and bottom bars after I saw "Minimal"
    by Calin Tataru. The color scheme comes from "Crisp" by Thibault NORMAND.

    Syntax highlighting uses the Autumn, which is one of the base color schemes
    included with Pygments

    Hugo Zen - https://github.com/rakuishi/hugo-zen
    Minimal  - https://github.com/calintat/minimal
    Crisp    - https://github.com/Zenithar/hugo-theme-crisp

  [ Libraries & Frameworks ]
    Skeleton - http://getskeleton.com/

  [ Fonts ]
