Front End
---------

  Theme
    While the color scheme and most of the markup is my own, I drew inspiration 
    from Papyrus which is a Jekyll theme. Syntax highlighting uses the Base16 
    Eighties Dark color scheme.
    
    Papyrus              - https://github.com/hugoferreira/papyrus-theme
    Base16 Eighties Dark - https://github.com/chriskempson/base16

  Libraries & Frameworks
    Bootstrap     - https://getbootstrap.com
    Normalize.css - https://necolas.github.io/normalize.css
    jQuery        - https://jquery.com
    
  Fonts
    PT Serif - https://fonts.google.com/specimen/PT+Serif
    Raleway  - https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Raleway
    Hack     - https://sourcefoundry.org/hack
